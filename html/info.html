info

a series of puzzles was devised, planned, and created over the spring break of 2017, mostly due to a lack of other things to do.

there is no particular target audience aside from my peers—that is, the puzzles are not unreasonably difficult and should not require any in-depth domain-specific knowledge to solve. however, they will likely start off relatively easy and get progressively more difficult.

none of the puzzles are lateral-thinking puzzles; I'll never be trying to "trick" you. they will all have solutions that can be logically justified.

the primary goal here is the enjoyment of the puzzle-solver, but whether there will be a prize for the first person to get to the end is yet to be determined.

collaboration is technically not forbidden, but I strongly suggest that you try the puzzles for yourself. (and if there is a prize, everyone else is your opponent.)

if you haven't solved a puzzle for longer than a day, I will give you a hint if you show me the progress you've made so far, even if it's just wild speculation.

finally, thanks to Lincoln for inspiring this project, and to you for your interest in it.

—Andy
